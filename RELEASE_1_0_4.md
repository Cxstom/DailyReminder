# v1.4 — Cooking & Fishing Dailies

### New Categories

- **Cooking Dailies** — Tracks the Shattrath cooking daily quests:
  - Manalicious
  - Revenge is Tasty
  - Soup for the Soul
  - Super Hot Stew

- **Fishing Dailies** — Tracks the Shattrath fishing daily quests:
  - Bait Bandits
  - Crocolisks in the City
  - Felblood Fillet
  - Shrimpin' Ain't Easy
  - The One That Got Away

### Settings

Both categories are **enabled by default** and have their own toggle checkboxes in:
- The first-run setup wizard
- The addon settings panel (Game Menu → Options → AddOns → Daily Reminder)
