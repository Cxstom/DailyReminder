# v1.1 — Initial Release

A lightweight TBC addon that reminds you to turn in completed daily quests before they reset.

### Highlights

- Tracks **Normal Dungeon**, **Heroic Dungeon**, and **PvP (Call to Arms)** dailies
- Three alert styles: **Chat Message**, **Raid Warning Text**, or **Popup Window**
- Six sound choices: Raid Warning, Ready Check, Alarm Clock, Spell Book, Gnome Alert Bot, Malacrass Warning (or none)
- Draggable **minimap button** — left-click for settings, right-click to check now
- **Dismiss** a reminder for the current session; it comes back next login
- Slash commands: `/dr`, `/dr check`, `/dr resume`, `/dr minimap`
- Saved variables persist your settings across sessions
